Our Payment Provider

All payment information labeled {% include "payments/stripe_bage.html" %} is securely stored with our payment provider, Stripe. Stripe is fully compliant with industry security standards (certified PCI Level 1 Service Provider) and used by companies and nonprofits alike (e.g. Unicef, Booking.com, Indiegogo). We do not have a copy of your payment method (e.g. credit card number, card expiry date, CVV or bank account details on our server. To allow us to issue donation receipts, we store a copy of your payment history on our server. This only includes required data as outlined in our privacy policy.
